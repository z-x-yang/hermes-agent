# Evelyn Visual Identity

The canonical character reference is the Evelyn Discord bot avatar (`1473189854266261604`), snapshotted for this asset set on 2026-07-13. Do not substitute a generic female portrait or restore the former black-bob/headband character.

## Locked identity anchors

- long hair with a thick side braid over the shoulder;
- crescent-moon hair clip at the temple;
- large lavender Japanese-anime eyes and the cheek beauty mark from the Discord reference;
- the reference face and expression without age-up, maturity, realism, or cuteness reinterpretation;
- pale lavender blouse with a dark charcoal collar and ribbon;
- no `Hermes`, `H`, `N`, caduceus, or other product lettering inside the artwork.

## Asset roles

- `assets/icon.png`, `.icns`, `.ico`: monochrome near-profile portrait looking viewer-left. It is redrawn as flat pure-black shapes, clean white negative space, and sparse graphite linework—not a desaturated color portrait. The former app icon supplies only this graphic language; the Discord avatar remains the sole character authority.
- `public/evelyn-brand-mark.png`: transparent color front portrait for 56px in-app tiles. It intentionally shares the character but not the exact composition of the system icon.
- `hermes_cli.banner.EVELYN_PORTRAIT`: compact left-facing gold/bronze terminal portrait derived from the monochrome icon; the moon glyph is preserved as an identity anchor.

Desktop and Bootstrap installer platform icon families must be regenerated from the same monochrome master. Their in-app brand marks must use the same transparent color asset.
